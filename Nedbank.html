Add an international recipient

I was permanently employed by DVT and outsourced to Nedbank, where I collaborated with the Forex team to deliver impactful design solutions. One of my key projects involved designing the user flow for adding international recipients on the Nedbank MoneyApp and website. This required executing the full UX process, including conducting user research and competitive analysis, creating low, medium, and high-fidelity mockups in Sketch, performing user testing, and collaborating closely with the broader design team to ensure alignment and consistency. During this time, I also developed a comprehensive understanding of Forex terminology, acronyms, and the specific regulatory and operational requirements essential to the bank’s processes, enabling me to design solutions that met both user and business needs. Project Summary

The high value payments team is required to create a platform for their staff to process incoming and outgoing payments. The International Standard Organisation is requiring all banks to adopt ISO2022 and a new MX format which is applicable to SWIFT messages. The project focuses on simplifying and unifying the financial sector and global business communication. This is done by introducing universally applicable terminology, which will enhances international payments and reduces risk.
