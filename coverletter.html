Motivational letter

"If you set your goals ridiculously high and it's a failure,
you will fail above everyone else's success." - James Cameron

Dear Sir/Madam

I am currently permanently employed by DVT, and am outsourced to a client (Nedbank) to provide design solutions for the Forex banking industry.

I view myself as being a creative person. I have always been able to think laterally. My studies have made me more curious and taught me to question that which is generally accepted - to not only adopt a different point of view, but to substantiate it, and to formulate new ways of problem solving. I believe my studies, together with my innate talents, will make me a valuable member of a creative team in the work environment.

Despite my rather eccentric spirit, I am, somewhat paradoxically, also extremely organised and fastidiously neat and tidy. I am full of energy and enthusiasm when working hard towards achieving my goals. I will extend this to the goals of my workplace. I believe my current exposure has taught me about the realities and challenges of the workplace. I hope that, through continous hard work, I will have the opportunity to continue learning and refine my knowledge, skills and develop further understanding of what is required of me in the commercial world.

If you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to contact me.
Please have a look and let me know.

Many thanks and best regards,

Victoria Thompson
